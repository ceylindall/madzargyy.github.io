Why is Dental Health Important?

Dental health is essential for overall well-being. It’s important to brush your teeth regularly, eat healthy foods, and visit the dentist frequently to maintain a healthy smile.

Tips for Healthy Teeth

  • Brush your teeth twice a day with fluoride toothpaste.
  • Floss daily to remove plaque between your teeth.
  • Visit your dentist regularly for check-ups.
